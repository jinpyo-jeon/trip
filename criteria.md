# Trip Activity Filtering Criteria

## Exclusions

1. **No Restaurants** - Category = "Restaurant" removed (have own food at accommodation)

2. **No Snow/Ice Activities** - Removed due to no snow confirmed:
   - Mohonk Mountain House
   - Hunter Mountain (Skiing)
   - Belleayre Mountain (Skiing)
   - Brewster Ice Arena
   - Carriage Lounge (Mohonk)
   - Ice Fishing (Ashokan)
   - Hunter Mountain Snow Tubing
   - Ice Fishing (Bashakill)

3. **Drive time limits**
   - **Default max: 40 min** - For short activities (1-2 hours)
   - **Up to 60-70 min OK** - If activity is several hours or full day (worth the drive)

## Scoring Formula

Weighted average of 5 factors:
- **Bachelor Party Fit (1-10): 50%** ← dominant factor
- Uniqueness vs NYC (1-10): 20%
- Relaxation (1-10): 10%
- Value for Money (1-10): 10%
- Convenience (1-10): 10%
