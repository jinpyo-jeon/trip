## Additional Activities (NEW)

### Tuthilltown Spirits Distillery (Gardiner, NY)
- **Location:** 14 Grist Mill Ln, Gardiner, NY *(18 miles / 25 min)*
- **Cost:** $30/person tour + tasting, $50/person cocktail workshop
- **Hours:** Mon, Wed-Sun 12pm-6pm (Thu-Sun until 8pm), Closed Tue
- **Details:** First whiskey distillery in NY since Prohibition. 45-min tour includes rick house visit + 6-sample tasting with souvenir rocks glass. Hands-on cocktail workshop available.
- **Website:** [Tuthilltown Spirits](https://www.tuthilltowndistillery.com/)

### SafeShoot Indoor Shooting Range (Kingston, NY)
- **Location:** 10 Kieffer Ln, Kingston, NY *(6 miles / 12 min)*
- **Phone:** (845) 443-7727
- **Hours:** Mon-Fri 10am-6pm, Sat 9am-5pm, Sun 10am-4pm
- **Details:** 24 state-of-the-art lanes across 3 indoor ranges. Gun rentals available. Available for group parties.
- **Website:** [SafeShoot](https://www.safeshootny.com/)

### Belleayre Mountain Skiing/Snowboarding
- **Location:** Highmount, NY *(45 miles / 1 hr)*
- **Cost:** Day passes ~$85-100, rentals extra
- **Details:** 55 trails, 1,404 ft vertical drop. Catskills' best skiing. On-mountain bar at Overlook Lodge.
- **Note:** Purchase tickets online in advance
- **Website:** [Belleayre Mountain](https://www.belleayre.com/)

### Newburgh Brewing Company
- **Location:** 88 S Colden St, Newburgh, NY *(28 miles / 40 min)*
- **Hours:** Wed-Thu 4-10/11pm, Fri 4-11pm, Sat 12-11pm, Sun 12-6pm
- **Details:** 4th floor taproom with sweeping Hudson River views. 20+ craft beers on tap. Great food menu (burgers, nachos, wings). Trivia Wed, Karaoke Thu, Live Music Fri-Sun.
- **Website:** [Newburgh Brewing](https://newburghbrewing.com/)

### Arrowood Farms Brewery & Distillery (Accord, NY)
- **Location:** 236 Lower Whitfield Rd, Accord, NY *(13 miles / 20 min)*
- **Winter Hours:** Saturdays only 12-9pm (Dec 1 - Mar 14)
- **Details:** Sustainable farm brewery with on-site distillery. All ingredients locally sourced. Great farmhouse ales and comfort food.
- **Website:** [Arrowood Farms](https://arrowoodfarms.com/)

### Suarez Family Brewery (Hudson, NY)
- **Location:** 2278 U.S. 9, Hudson, NY *(33 miles / 43 min)*
- **Hours:** Thu-Sun 12-6pm
- **Details:** Highly acclaimed craft brewery specializing in lagers and mixed fermentation ales. Beautiful tasting room. "Best pilsner anywhere" per reviews.
- **Website:** [Suarez Family Brewery](https://www.drinksuarez.com/)

### Hutton Brickyards (Kingston, NY) *(6 miles / 12 min)*
- **Details:** 100-acre riverfront venue with cooking classes (pasta, pizza, charcuterie), cocktail bar, and fireside lounging. Great for groups.
- **Phone:** (845) 514-4853
- **Website:** [Hutton Brickyards](https://www.huttonbrickyards.com/)

### Brewery/Distillery Bus Tours
- **The Little Beer Bus:** $155-160/person for full-day tours hitting 2-3 breweries/distilleries. Includes tastings, lunch, and transport.
- **Website:** [The Little Beer Bus](https://thelittlewinebus.com/sites/the-little-beer-bus/)

### Mid-Hudson Sporting Clays
- **Details:** 32 shooting stations on a world-class sporting clays course. Great for groups, all skill levels welcome.
- **Website:** [Mid-Hudson Sporting Clays](https://midhudsonsporting.com/)

### Resorts World Catskills Casino (Monticello, NY)
- **Location:** Monticello, NY *(48 miles / 55 min)*
- **Details:** Full casino with 18-table poker room (Texas Hold'em, Omaha, Stud), 150 table games, 2,150 slots, sportsbook. 65" TVs at every poker seat. Bars, restaurants, and live entertainment on-site.
- **Website:** [Resorts World Catskills](https://rwcatskills.com/casino/poker/)

### Comedy at Assembly Kingston
- **Location:** Uptown/Stockade District, Kingston *(6 miles / 12 min)*
- **Details:** Kingston's newest concert venue. Stand-up comedy showcases regularly scheduled. Check for shows on your dates.
- **Note:** Squalid Gold Comedy Showcase on Jan 23, 2026 (Day 2 of your trip!)

### Tubby's Open Mic Comedy Night

### Ice Fishing (Weather Permitting)
- **Best Spots Near You:**
  - Ashokan Reservoir *(12 miles / 20 min)* - Lake trout, smallmouth bass
  - Lake Taghkanic State Park *(30 miles / 40 min)* - 168 acres, 1.5-mile lake
  - Bashakill Marsh, Wurtsboro *(35 miles / 45 min)*
- **Requirements:** Valid NY fishing license required. Check ice thickness (min 3-4 inches solid ice)
- **Info:** [NY DEC Ice Fishing](https://dec.ny.gov/things-to-do/ice-fishing)

### Hunter Mountain Skiing/Snowboarding
- **Location:** Hunter, NY *(38 miles / 50 min)*
- **Details:** 1,600 ft vertical drop, 58 trails, 100% snowmaking coverage. Steeper than Belleayre. Snow tubing park (largest in NY). On Epic Pass.
- **Cost:** Lift tickets vary - buy in advance online
- **Note:** Tickets are limited, pre-purchase required
- **Website:** [Hunter Mountain](https://www.huntermtn.com/)
